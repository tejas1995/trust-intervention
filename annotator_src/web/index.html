AI Confidence

  • In this experiment, your task is to evaluate an AI model and judge whether it is correct or not based on its confidence.
  • After your decision, you may place a bet based on how certain you are.
  • If your decision regarding the AI system is correct, you get the bet amount but if it is incorrect, you lose it.
  • Your payment is $1 plus whatever you accumulate during this experiment (possible overall minimum is $1).
