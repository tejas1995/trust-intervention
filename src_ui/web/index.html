Welcome to our user study. You will be trying to answer questions with the assistance of an AI system. We will first walk you through an example question with images and afterwards you will annotate 60 examples.
Each example consists of a question, the AI answer and the AI confidence.
Your task is to decide whether the AI system is correct or incorrect, by looking at the AI confidence.
After your decision, you place a bet based on how sure you are of your decision. If you are very sure, place a larger bet and vice versa if not.
If your decision about the correctness of the AI system is correct, you receive money and you lose it if your decision was wrong. Therefore, for each question, you can increase your balance by up to $0.1, or decrease it by upto $0.1 (your total balance will not fall below $0 though).

The guaranteed minimum is $1.0 regardless of your performance, but you can earn more money if your bets are correct (the additional money will be paid out as bonuses).
Our AI system encounters diverse types of questions.

You are not permitted to leave this tab for the duration of the experiment. This will be monitored and violation of this can result in your submission being rejected. The average time of the experiment is 5 minutes.

You will not be paid if you already participated in this study before.
